Enable WebGL for Splat and Treat

WebGL is required to view 3D worlds. Follow the instructions below to enable it in your browser.

Chrome / Edge
Firefox
Safari

Enable Hardware Acceleration in Chrome/Edge

  1. Open Chrome or Edge settings by typing chrome://settings/system in the address bar
  2. Find the "System" section
  3. Toggle ON "Use hardware acceleration when available"
  4. Click "Relaunch" to restart your browser
  5. Return to Splat and Treat

Enable Hardware Acceleration in Firefox

  1. Open Firefox preferences by typing about:preferences in the address bar
  2. Scroll down to the "Performance" section
  3. Uncheck "Use recommended performance settings"
  4. Check "Use hardware acceleration when available"
  5. Restart Firefox
  6. Return to Splat and Treat

Enable WebGL in Safari

  1. Open Safari Preferences
  2. Go to the "Advanced" tab
  3. Check "Show Develop menu in menu bar"
  4. Click "Develop" in the menu bar
  5. Go to "Experimental Features"
  6. Ensure "WebGL 2.0" is enabled
  7. Restart Safari
  8. Return to Splat and Treat
